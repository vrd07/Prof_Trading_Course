## 🎯 Learning Objectives

*By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:*
- Identify the major participant groups in Forex
- Understand what motivates each participant to trade
- Explain why this hierarchy changes how you read price

---

## 📖 LESSON CONTENT

### SECTION 1 — You Are Never Trading Alone

Every time you click buy or sell, someone is on the other side — often an institution with different information and incentives.

---

### SECTION 2 — The Forex Market Hierarchy

The market is a hierarchy:

- **Central Banks** (policy + interventions)
- **Commercial & Investment Banks** (market making + order flow)
- **Hedge Funds** (aggressive speculation)
- **Corporations** (hedging + conversion)
- **Retail traders** (smallest, most predictable)

---

### SECTION 3 — The Broker: Your Gateway (and Their Business Model)

Brokers are intermediaries. Some route orders (ECN/STP); others internalize flow (market makers). Regulation matters.

---

### SECTION 4 — Why Knowing the Players Gives You an Edge

Stop hunts / liquidity sweeps are a mechanical consequence of how large orders get filled.

  title="The Stop Hunt Sequence"

## ✅ KEY TAKEAWAYS

- Different participants trade for different reasons
- Central banks set the macro backdrop
- Banks and funds can exploit clustered retail behavior
